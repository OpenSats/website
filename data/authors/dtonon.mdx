Daniele is an entrepreneur who grew up in the web space starting in the late
90s, developing e-commerce platforms, online services and management software
with the goal of bringing value to businesses and improving the final users'
experience with helpful and pleasant tools.

From mid-2022 on, he is following Nostr's development, where he is actively
involved in the design of major clients, most notably [Gossip](https://github.com/mikedilger/gossip) and [Coracle.social](https://coracle.social), as
well as various tools such as the [Njump](https://github.com/fiatjaf/njump) static content gateway[^1] that is live on
[njump.me](https://njump.me).

He is passionate about design, user experience and software development, he puts
accessibility, sustainability and privacy as the core values of his activities.

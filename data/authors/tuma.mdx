Tuma is an open-source writer and engineer based in Italy. He contributes to
[Bitcoin Optech](https://bitcoinops.org/), writes "Last Week in Bitcoin" for
[BTC++ Insider Edition](https://insider.btcpp.dev/), and publishes
[Inside Bitcoin Code](https://tuma.substack.com/), a weekly series on Bitcoin
Core for enthusiasts without programming experience. His background in
bioengineering and C++ gives him the technical depth to follow developer calls
and mailing lists, and the ability to turn what he finds there into writing
that anyone can follow.

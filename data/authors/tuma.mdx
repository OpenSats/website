Tuma is an open-source writer and engineer based in Italy. He contributes to
Bitcoin Optech and writes "Last Week in Bitcoin" for BTC++ Insider Edition,
covering the Bitcoin development ecosystem week by week. His background in
bioengineering and C++ gives him the technical depth to follow developer calls
and mailing lists, and the ability to turn what he finds there into writing
that anyone can follow.

Tuma is an open-source writer and engineer from Italy. He contributes to
[Bitcoin Optech](https://bitcoinops.org/) and writes "Last Week in Bitcoin" for
[bitcoin++ Insider Edition](https://insider.btcpp.dev/), covering the Bitcoin
development ecosystem week by week. He also publishes
[Inside Bitcoin Code](https://insidebitcoincode.substack.com/) and built
[Sats Tracker](https://github.com/TumaBitcoiner/sats-tracker), a React Native
expense tracker for people on a Bitcoin standard. He also writes
[The ContextVM World](https://tumabitcoiner.github.io/cvm.html), a biweekly
series on ContextVM, MCP, and Nostr.

His background in bioengineering and C++ gives him the technical depth to follow
developer calls and mailing lists, and the ability to turn what he finds there
into writing that anyone can follow. When he's not writing, you'll probably find
him posting live updates from a developer conference on
[Nostr](https://njump.to/npub1jeqhakwnjf3hp2y8gv8uly95d7asn64y3y0wex7usl5sye4s9f2sgznmal).

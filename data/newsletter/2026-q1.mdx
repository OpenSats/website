## Support this work

OpenSats is a [501(c)(3) public charity](https://opensats.org/transparency).
100% of donations to our funds go directly to open-source projects; our
[Operations Budget](/funds/ops) is kept strictly separate. If you'd like to
help sustain this work, please consider setting up a recurring donation.

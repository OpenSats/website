Sometime in early 2026, a researcher replicated a live-network attack that
lets outside observers correlate the same Bitcoin node across Tor and
clearnet — then [opened a pull
request](https://github.com/bitcoin/bitcoin/pull/33498) with a proposed
mitigation. [We funded her in
March](/blog/five-grants-to-strengthen-bitcoin-development#naiyoma). She had
been at it well before we paid her, and she'll be at it well after. That's a
surprisingly normal week around here.

- **A shipped piece of invisible infrastructure.** Cody, a returning Nostr
  Fund grantee, extracted his relay's core into a standalone TypeScript
  [framework](https://github.com/CodyTseng/nostr-relay); one-click personal
  relays now build on it. Most nostr users will never hear the name, and
  that's the point.
- **A threshold crossed.** We passed **390 grants awarded** this quarter.
  The first one went out in July 2023.
- **An ETF that kept its promise.** [Bitwise's BITB
  ETF](/blog/opensats-receives-donation-from-bitwise-etf) began recurring
  donations to our General Fund — part of a 10%-of-gross-profits pledge they
  made at launch. Brink and HRF's Bitcoin Development Fund get the other
  cuts.

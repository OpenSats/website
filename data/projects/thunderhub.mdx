## About this project

Send and receive both Lightning and Bitcoin payments in a simple and easy-to-use interface. Transaction reports provide a quick overview of invoices coming into your node and payments being made. Manage channels, forward payments, and much more using integrations with BTCPay, RaspiBlitz, MyNode, Voltage, Umbrel, and Start9.
